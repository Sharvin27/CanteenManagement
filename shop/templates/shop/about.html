    About Us

    Welcome to CRCE CANTEEN

    Welcome to CRCE Canteen, an online platform designed by Aditya, Janet, and Sharvin to bring delicious food right to your fingertips. With our user-friendly website, teachers can now easily order food from the comfort of their classrooms, eliminating the need for long queues and wait times. This online canteen management system not only saves time and effort but also helps in tracking and managing inventory, reducing waste, and increasing profits. We look forward to serving you and thank you for choosing CRCE Canteen!
